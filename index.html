Hello, my name is Omar Perez.
I am currently a senior in Computer Science at Arizona State University.

Going into college, I had no idea what career I wanted to pursue. As a freshman, I came to ASU under an Exploratory major. My advisor, then informed me that I could take a programming class instead of taking a science. At the time, my only exposure to any kind of programming was seeing one of my teammates programming our robot in my high school robotics team. With her suggestion, I took CSE 110, an introductory level Java class. This class gave me a fascination with programming and made me realize that it was the career that I wanted to pursue.

My other hobbies include: playing and watching soccer, learning new languages, and learning to play instruments. I also currently compete with ASU's dragon boat team.
